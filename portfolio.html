OsqledARen

Cozy Corners

Cozy Corners

Cozy Corners makes sharing your favourit places easier. A user can add a new "cozy corner" to a map by filling in the longitude and latitude. A description, images and tags can also be added.

Cozy Corners is the outcome from a group project of three peopel in the course Mobile Development with Web Technologies at KTH.

scrollr

We created scroller as an alternative to regular scrolling. By tilting your phone quickly toward yourself, the socialmedia flow, like Instagram or Facebook, scrolls automatically and no hancs are needed.
